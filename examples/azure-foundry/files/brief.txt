Design a minimal experiment about belief revision in a social feed.

Constraints:
- Use a pre-registered factual question with an initially ambiguous evidence set.
- Introduce one clearly sourced piece of contrary evidence after an initial answer.
- Define at least one quantitative measure of belief change.
- Include a control condition.
- Keep the protocol small enough to reproduce on a laptop with API access.

The group should leave the round with a concrete hypothesis and next step.
