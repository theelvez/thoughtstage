The research contract

Boundaries you can test,
not merely trust.

01

One shared premise

Every participant receives the same experiment-level system prompt, byte for byte.

02

Independent minds

Each agent may use its own provider, model, parameters, tools, and credential reference.

03

Sealed soliloquies

A private reflection is visible to the researcher and never enters another agent’s context.

04

Reproducible evidence

Runs preserve configuration, provenance, inputs, costs, and separately typed event streams.
